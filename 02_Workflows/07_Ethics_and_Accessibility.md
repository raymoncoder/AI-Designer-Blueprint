# ⚖️ Part 7: Ethics & Accessibility

With great AI power comes a massive responsibility. In 2026, AI can accidentally generate biased interfaces or inaccessible flows faster than ever before. 

**Core Responsibilities:**
1. **The "Human-in-the-Loop" Mandate:** AI is a powerful intern, not the CEO. You must critically review every generated insight, design, and line of code.
2. **Bias Auditing:** AI models are trained on historical data, which contains systemic biases. Ensure your user personas and generative outputs represent diverse, inclusive demographics.
3. **WCAG Automation:** Use AI to your advantage to *enforce* accessibility. Set up your AI agents to automatically flag insufficient color contrast, missing keyboard navigation states, and improper screen-reader labeling.
