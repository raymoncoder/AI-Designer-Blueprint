# 🧩 Part 2: Prompt Engineering vs. Context Engineering

In 2024, designers obsessed over "magic prompts." In April 2026, **Prompt Engineering is a legacy skill**. The focus has shifted to **Context Engineering**.

- **What is Context Engineering?** 
  It is the art of providing AI agents (like Gemini 3.1 or Antigravity) with structured, comprehensive information about your product so they can make autonomous, accurate decisions.
- **The Context Stack:**
  1. **Brand Identity:** JSON files containing tone of voice, hex codes, typography rules.
  2. **Design System:** Design tokens, component constraints, API hooks.
  3. **User Personas:** Deeply documented pain points, technical literacy, and goals.
  4. **Business Logic:** Edge cases, technical constraints, monetization strategy.

**How to Apply It:**
Instead of saying: *"Design a modern login screen,"* you provide the Context Stack and say: *"Execute the login flow per our design system, handling the edge cases outlined in auth-rules.md, and ensure the tone matches our enterprise B2B persona."*
